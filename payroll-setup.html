CSV Upload

Export a payroll CSV from any system and upload it here. Burse will parse employee names, emails, and salary data automatically.

Required CSV format

employee_number,full_name,email,gross_salary,net_salary,department,job_title,id_number,phone,payday,start_date EMP001,Nandi Mokoena,nandi@company.co.za,18500,14200,Retail,Sales Rep,9001015009087,0821234567,25,2024-01-15 EMP002,Thabo Khumalo,thabo@company.co.za,32000,24600,Finance,Accountant,8803125009080,0837654321,25,2023-06-01
Columns: full_name and gross_salary are required. All others are optional.
